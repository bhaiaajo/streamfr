DMCA Compliance

At Stream FR, we respect the rights of content owners and are committed to following copyright law. Please note that we do not host any media files directly on our servers. All media content available via Stream FR is provided by third-party hosting services. Stream FR functions solely as an aggregator, linking users to content hosted externally.

If you are a copyright owner and believe that content accessible through Stream FR infringes upon your rights, we recommend contacting the hosting provider directly to request content removal. Unfortunately, Stream FR cannot remove, modify, or control any files hosted on third-party servers.

However, if you would like us to remove links to specific content from Stream FR, please reach out to us via our official DMCA contact email or through our contact page with the following information:

  • Your contact details, including name, address, and email.
  • A clear description of the copyrighted content and URLs you wish to have removed.
  • A statement affirming your good faith belief that the content is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • Proof of copyright ownership or authorization to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

Upon receipt of your complete DMCA notice, we will review the request and take appropriate action to remove or restrict access to the requested content where possible. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in helping us maintain compliance with copyright laws.
