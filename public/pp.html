Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to **StreamFR**, a platform designed for streaming movies and TV shows through third-party embedded content. Protecting your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information collected, how it's handled, and your rights. Please note that as an open-source platform, we respect transparency, and no personal data is stored by us directly. All data collection and storage are managed by third-party services. We encourage you to read the entire policy thoroughly.

Overview of Tools Used

At **StreamFR**, we use several tools to enhance your user experience, monitor website traffic, and serve relevant ads. Below is a breakdown of the tools in use:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us track and analyze website traffic without collecting personal information.
  • Adsterra & RollerAds: Third-party ad networks that serve advertisements based on user interactions.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Provides insights into how users navigate our website, including heatmaps and session replays. This helps us improve usability and performance.

Third-Party Embedded Content and Cookies

Movies and TV shows on **StreamFR** are embedded from third-party providers. These providers may place cookies on your device to track your progress (e.g., where you paused a video) and serve targeted advertisements. **StreamFR** does not control or have access to this data. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party platforms for detailed information on their data practices.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website performance, user behavior, and engagement. The information collected is anonymous and includes details such as page views, session duration, and the general location of users. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored by **StreamFR**. The data is processed by Google under its privacy policy, and you can choose to opt-out of this data collection by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Adsterra and RollerAds

Ads on **StreamFR** are delivered through Adsterra and RollerAds. These third-party ad networks use cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your interactions and browsing habits. We do not have access to or control over these cookies. These partners may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing history to deliver relevant ads. For more information, please refer to their respective privacy policies on how they handle data collection and processing.

Microsoft Clarity

Microsoft Clarity helps us understand user behavior on our website by providing analytics such as heatmaps and session replays. This tool allows us to identify which parts of our website are being used and optimize it for a better experience. Clarity collects anonymized data to ensure that we never store any personal information about you. For more details on how Microsoft handles data, please refer to the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Continue Watching Feature

The "Continue Watching" feature allows you to pick up a movie or show where you left off. This information is stored locally on your device in the browser's localStorage. No data is shared with us or third parties. However, third-party video hosts may also use cookies to store your viewing progress, so they may have their own systems for handling this data.

Because You Watched

The "Because You Watched" feature utilizes the **TMDB API** to recommend content similar to what you last watched. No personal data is collected or tracked by **StreamFR** for this feature. It is purely based on the last movie or show you watched, and the recommendations are pulled directly from TMDB's library of content. You can learn more about TMDB's data practices here.
