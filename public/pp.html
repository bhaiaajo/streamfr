Stream FR Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Stream FR, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our platform. Stream FR does not collect or store any personal data. Instead, certain third-party services collect and store data on our behalf.
We are also an open-source platform licensed under the Eclipse Public License - v 2.0.

2. Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Stream FR does not collect or store personal data. However, the following third-party services collect data to provide analytics and advertisements:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymized information such as pages viewed, session duration, and geographic locations.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records session replays, clicks, scroll patterns, and generates heatmaps for analyzing user behavior.
  • Adsterra Ads: Displays personalized advertisements based on your browsing history through the use of cookies.

Please review their respective privacy policies to understand how they collect and use your data:
Google Analytics Privacy Policy
Microsoft Clarity Privacy Policy
Adsterra Privacy Policy

3. LocalStorage and "Continue Watching"

The "continue watching" feature stores data locally in your browser’s localStorage. This data is stored on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data by clearing your browser’s localStorage.

4. "Because You Watched" Feature

We use the TMDB API to display similar movies to the ones you last watched. This feature does not recommend content; it fetches related movies based on your last watched items stored locally. We do not store any data for this feature.
For more information about TMDB, you can review their privacy policy: TMDB Privacy Policy.

5. Use of Cookies

Cookies are used by third-party services (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Adsterra) to track anonymized data and serve personalized advertisements.

  • Analytics: Cookies help Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity track pages visited, user interaction, and performance analytics.
  • Advertising: Adsterra uses cookies to display ads relevant to your interests. These cookies are managed by Adsterra, not by Stream FR.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may impact certain functionalities of the website.

6. Data Security

While we do not store data ourselves, the third-party services we use (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Adsterra) take necessary steps to ensure the protection of data they collect. We encourage you to review their security practices by reading their privacy policies linked above.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you can manage your data preferences:

  • Opt-Out of Cookies: Manage cookie settings via your browser to limit or prevent cookies from tracking your activity.
  • Data Deletion: You can request data deletion directly from third-party services like Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Adsterra.
  • Do Not Track: You can enable the "Do Not Track" setting in your browser to minimize data collection by third parties.

8. Open Source Licensing

Stream FR is open source and licensed under the Eclipse Public License - v 2.0, which allows for the free use, modification, and distribution of the website's code.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or services. Any updates will be posted here with the revised effective date.
